History of our company

The company started in the 19th century as a seller of logarithmic rulers. At the time, logarithmic rulers were a vital tool for scientists and engineers, as they allowed for complex calculations to be made quickly and easily. The company became well-known for its high-quality rulers and built a strong reputation in the industry.

To survive and thrive in this new digital landscape, the company transformed its business model and began to offer middleware server support to businesses. Today, the company is a leading provider of middleware server support and related services. Its solutions are used by businesses of all sizes and across a wide range of industries, from healthcare to finance to manufacturing. The company continues to innovate and evolve its products and services to meet the changing needs of its customers, while staying true to its roots in precision measurement and data analysis.
